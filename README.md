Nusantara Quest: A Rogue-like History Adventure
Welcome to Nusantara Quest, a rogue-like game designed to test your tactical skills and your knowledge of Indonesian history and culture. Navigate through the archipelago, face legendary foes, and reclaim the lost scrolls of knowledge.

Game Overview
In this journey, you play as a Wanderer traveling through different eras—from the ancient maritime kingdoms of Majapahit and Sriwijaya to the pivotal moments of the Proclamation. Every floor is a new challenge where the layout, enemies, and trivia questions change dynamically.

Key Features
Rouge-like features

Cultural Trivia: Fight enemies to gain power-ups by answering questions about Indonesian heroes, geography, and local wisdom.

Procedural Maps: No two runs are the same, explore different islands with unique biomes.

Artifact System: Collect items to boost your stats.
